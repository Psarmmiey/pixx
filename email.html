Survey: tell us your JAMstack feedback!

Help the JAMstack community by giving us your feedback in the first State of the JAMstack community survey. When a community is growing this fast, it's hard to know what's standard practice and what's a niche interest. Tell us your JAMstack feedback and technology choices, so all can benefit from the results — newcomers and experienced users alike. The survey takes 15 minutes and is anonymous unless you want to be contacted. The survey closes April 19, and results will be shared during the JAMstack Conf virtual event.


New insights available in Fricapix Analytics

Fricapix Analytics just got better. New features include a geography view, so you can quickly see which country is driving the most traffic, and improved time-based filtering. Head to the docs to learn more. To try Analytics, log into your account, select one of your sites, and then navigate to the Analytics tab to enable it for $9/site/month.
